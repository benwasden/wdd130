Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the rafting website is to make it easy for customers to browse for the rafting trip they want to go on and book their vacation. The location, length of trip, and price will all be shown, alongside more information about the advertised location.

Audience

Who are the target customers? They are people who want to spend time outdoors and want to spend some of their vacation time rafting with their friends or family. Rather than just appealing to affluent individuals, there will be a variety of trips at different price points to pick from. What questions will be answered by them accessing the website? The website will answer questions like price point, location, how long a trip will last, and more. What do they want? They want to get information to help plan their rafting vacation.
